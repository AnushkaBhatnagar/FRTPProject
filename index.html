You Matter!

Mental health is essential for leading a happy life. It affects how we feel, think, and live our lives by encompassing our emotional, psychological, and sociological well-being. Prioritizing mental health is critical.

You Matter collates the necessary resources one needs so that help is just a click away. Please note that some contacts listed on our site are specific to India.

Our Resources

Self-Care

Mental self care involves activities that help declutter your mind and reduce your stress levels.

Reach Out

Asking for help with your mental health can be hard, but you are not alone.

Online Mediums

Online therapy has proven to help those experiencing isolation, depression, and anxiety.

Spread Awareness

Raising community awareness of mental health issues helps eliminate the stigma and makes it easier for people to seek help.
